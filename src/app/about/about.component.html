About Us

Our Vision

A strong technical volunteering community with social good missions.

Our Mission

To promote and support technical volunteering for social good, with the objective of increasing social impact and fostering personal development through volunteering.

Our Values

SUPPORT Foster a strong, supportive community within the diverse volunteer pool
