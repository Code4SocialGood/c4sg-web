About Us

our vision

Connecting nonprofit companies with great volunteers

As a nonprofit, it can be hard to offer paid work. A limited budget means fewer workers, which in turn means work that cannot always be done.

At Code for Social Good, we understand the struggle of nonprofit companies. And as a nonprofit company ourselves, we seek to provide other such organizations with the technical help they need-for no charge whatsoever.

our mission

To promote the social good through volunteering

Code for Social Good is a volunteer community that seeks to promote social welfare by supporting nonprofits in need. We appreciate the cause your company represents and we want to be able to help you continue your mission.

Code for Social Good is made up of volunteers from across the tech industry. We are technical writers, coders and programmers who are ready to assist you with the tech services you require. Simply submit a request and we will connect you with the help you need.

our values

The core of what we are

Other than our awesome volunteers, we are best represented by our three values:

Community

A sense of community is important to our network. We support all our volunteers and we strive to build good relationships; between nonprofits, workers and both.

Service

We are a strong group of volunteers dedicated to promoting social good. A commitment to service is the glue that binds us together. Everyone is here to help.

Diversity

We match companies with volunteers of all age groups and experience levels. Our variety of skill sets and backgrounds helps ensure that each project receives the help it needs.

Join Us

A volunteer site made by volunteers

Beyond helping nonprofits, Code for Social Good exists to help its volunteers. Whether you’re an industry worker looking to help a good cause or a college student looking for experience, Code for Social Good has a place for you.

We are a site made for volunteers by volunteers. We rely on people like you to continue our mission and promote our values across the web.

Nonprofits interested in our services may submit a request in our ‘Projects’ section.
Volunteers may make a profile and then offer to help with these projects at their choosing.
