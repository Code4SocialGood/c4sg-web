VOLUNTEERS
Apply your technical skills
in service of others
Volunteers with C4SG provide free
assistance with tech projects to
nonprofits in need. As a volunteer,
you control what projects you take
and how you become involved with
those projects. Whatever your skill
and experience level, we will have a
project for you.


NONPROFITS
Find volunteer help for
tech projects and services
Nonprofits can register with C4SG to
obtain free volunteer help with tech
projects and services. Simply make a
listing and you will be free to reach
out to volunteers and select from a
list of qualified applicants. As an
organization, we are here to help you
help others.
